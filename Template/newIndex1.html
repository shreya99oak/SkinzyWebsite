What we do?

Skinzy Software Solutions is a Healthcare Research start-up. Skinzy provides a simple and effective solution to your skin-related diagnostic questions. Skinzy helps identify your skin within seconds.

How we do?

Our team designs various AI techniques involving image processing and machine learning for detection of skin diseases. A clinical trial validates the results of ech disease. DermaPhoto, a mobile appis used in the process of diagonsis.

Why we do?

Skinzy aspires for the betterment of society and does not intend to serve as a tool for preliminary diagnosis where the expertise of dermatologists is rarely available..
